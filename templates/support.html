Help Center

Welcome to the CollectiveRCM Help Center. This section is designed to assist all users in navigating the platform, resolving issues, and understanding system features.

Common Issues & Solutions

Issue Suggested Resolution Contact
Cannot log in Ensure credentials are correct. Try resetting your password or clearing your browser cache. intranet@collectivercm.com
Tasks not visible Check your role or team assignment. Some tasks are department-specific. Your department lead
Study materials not loading Ensure your browser supports PDF rendering. Check for access permissions under your role. Education Coordinator
Exam results missing Results may be pending admin review or are available under the “Dashboard” section. intranet@collectivercm.com
Incorrect profile data Edit via your profile page. If fields are locked or incorrect, contact the system administrators. System Admins

System Navigation Tips

  • Use the top navigation bar to access Profile, Tasks, Study Materials, and Management areas.
  • The dashboard shows active courses, deadlines, and announcements tailored to your role.
  • Use dark mode for long sessions using the moon/sun icon on the bottom right corner.
  • Use Ctrl + B to quickly toggle between light and dark mode.

If your issue persists, please escalate it via the Support page or reach out to your supervisor.
